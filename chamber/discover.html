Samson Siasia Sports Stadium

Samson Siasia Sports Stadium is a multi-use stadium located at Onopa-Ovom, Yenagoa. Previously known as the Yenagoa Township Stadium. It was rededicated in 2009 and was named after Samson Siasia, a former Nigerian football player who coached a number of teams and recorded a number of successes.

Peace Park

It serves as an amusement park, and thousands of people visit the park for vacation and recreational purposes, and it has served the needs of everyone within the state by its strategic location at the heart of the state capital. This is a popular resort and leisure centre in Bayelsa. Reputed to be the place where former Niger Delta militants in Bayelsa laid down their arms and renounced militancy, the park features an artificial lake, relaxation spot, and recreational facilities.

Ferry Terminal

The Yenagoa Ferry Terminal is located at Swali market, Yenagoa, Nigeria. Mostly used by Fishermen, lumberers and people travelling from Yenagoa to Nembe,Kiama, Amasomia, Brass or Ogbia.

Bayelsa Medical University

A public university situated at Amarata, Yenagoa Bayelsa State. It was estblished in August 2018 and has four faculties; Faculty of Clinical Sciences,Health Sciences, Basic Medical Sciences and Faculty of Sciences.

Edumanom Forest Reserve

Edumanom Forest Reserve is home to some of the last chimpanzees in Nigeria and covers part of the old Nembe Kingdom, now divided into the Nembe and Brass local government areas, in Bayelsa State. It is a freshwater swamp forest with an area of 9,324 hectares. It is known to be home to the endemic Sclater's guenon, olive colobus and Niger Delta red colobus.

Aridolf Resort Wellness & Spa

Sited at an ideal location in Ekeki, Yenagoa. A Resort with everything you need from hospitality, sports and entertainment facilities all in one place.

Royal Tulip Castle

Located just off D. S. P. Alamiesiegha Memorial Hall. This 4-star hotel provides 20 guestrooms each with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, climate control and a balcony for a pleasant stay in Yenagoa.

Kontiki Amusement Park

Located at No 12 Mein Boulus Street, by TB referral Hospital Yenegwe, Yenagoa, Bayelsa State. Kontiki Amusement Park is a family and kids friendly relaxation spot and amusement centre. If you are looking for a place to relax or hangout with your kids in Bayelsa, Kontiki Amusement Park is the place to visit.
