YenTown Yenagoa was formed in 2019 as a merger of the Yenagoa Chamber of Commerce, Economic Development Partnership, and Economic Development Foundation (Ascent Innovation). As the premier western South South economic development organization, YenTown is committed to promoting business and personal advancement through a targeted spotlight on jobs, infrastructure, and overall quality of life in and around the Black Hills region and the Yenagoa metro area. Together, we strive to embrace the heart and soul of our community through diversified growth and strong professional opportunities advocating the strengths of the Black Hills.
